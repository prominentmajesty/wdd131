Healthy Living Hub Website

Your Gateway to a Healthier Lifestyle

1. Site Name

Healthy Living Hub Website
This name was chosen because it clearly communicates the website’s focus on promoting healthy living and serves as a central hub for resources, tips, and community support related to wellness. It is memorable, descriptive, and inclusive for a broad audience interested in improving their health.
Optional domain availability: healthylivinghub.org

2. Site Purpose

The Healthy Living Hub Website aims to inspire and empower individuals to lead healthier lives by providing reliable information, practical tips, nutritious recipes, fitness routines, and a supportive community. The site will feature articles, guides, and interactive tools to help users make informed decisions about their health and wellness journey.

3. Scenarios

  • What are some easy and healthy recipes I can prepare for my family?
  • How can I start a beginner-friendly fitness routine at home?

4. Color Schema

Primary: #388E3C (Green) - Used for headers, buttons, and accents
Secondary: #F5F5F5 (Light Gray) - Used for backgrounds and sections
Accent: #FFA726 (Orange) - Used for highlights and call-to-action elements

The green color represents health and vitality, while the light gray ensures readability and a clean look. The orange accent adds energy and draws attention to important elements.

5. Typography

  • Heading Font: Montserrat – Used for all headings to provide a modern and clean appearance.
  • Body Font: Open Sans – Used for paragraphs and general content for readability.
